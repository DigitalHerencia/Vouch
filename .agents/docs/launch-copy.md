# Vouch Launch Copy

Source of truth date: 2026-04-30

## Approved Positioning

Vouch is a commitment-backed payment coordination system for pre-arranged appointments and in-person agreements.

Merchants and providers protect their time. Customers and clients show serious intent.

## Core Explanation

A merchant/provider creates a Vouch for a pre-arranged appointment or in-person agreement. The customer/client accepts and pays the customer total. If the release path succeeds, the merchant/provider receives the protected amount. If the release path does not complete, the payment is refunded, voided, or not captured according to provider state.

## Pricing Copy

Use:

- Protected amount
- Vouch service fee
- Processing fee offset
- Customer total
- Merchant receives

Avoid hiding fees or implying Vouch holds funds directly.

## Prohibited Copy

Do not call Vouch:

- escrow
- marketplace
- broker
- booking marketplace
- scheduler
- dispute resolution
- claims process
- review system
